\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
represents a unique city. 

The \doxylink{class_city}{City} class contains the \doxylink{class_position}{Position} of the ciy and a unique identifier (UID).

The UID is generated automatically by the class, and is used to identify the city. Two cities will always have different UIDs, even if they have the same position.
